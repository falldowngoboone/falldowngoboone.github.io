Hello. I'm Ryan.

I’m a front-end developer and designer based in Fort Worth, TX. I currently help make the space that comes from The Container Store. I also write things that occasionally make sense on Twitter and my personal blog, falldowngoboone.

History

Like most every other developers I've met, I started doing web development because I was in a band, but for about 15 years, I was professionally a graphic designer. A few years ago, I became seriously curious (or curiously serious?) about pursuing a full-time career as a developer. I had been doing freelance web design and development work on and off since college, but I decided to make it official. In July of 2017, I joined The Container Store's Technology department as an applications developer.

As a member of the TCS Technology team, I've facilitated our in-house JavaScript Special Interest Group and am an active member in our Technical Architecture Guild, a group that works to form architectural decisions department wide. I (really) love talking and have done so in the presence of other people, oddly enough, mostly about React and JavaScript. I'm currently interested in building beautiful, performant, and accessible UI in a maintainable way. Drop me a line if you want to know more, and follow me on Twitter for nonsense if you feel so inclined.
